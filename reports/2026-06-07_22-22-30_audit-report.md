## Summary

- **Counts:** Critical 0 · High 2 · Medium 5 · Low 7.
- **Top 3 high-priority fixes:**
  1. Reconcile the export **auto-split** claim vs reality in FEATURES_REQUIRED.md §5 (or wire the split).
  2. Cap actual per-member decompressed bytes in `archives.py` instead of trusting declared `file_size`.
  3. Clear the ESLint unused-`beforeEach` error.
- **Top 2 architectural improvements:**
  1. Make `en.js` an explicit spelling-neutral base and move **both** US and GB spelling deltas into their override files (remove the incidental-spelling dependency).
  2. Raise the untrusted-input boundary's test coverage (archives/converters/importer 50–66%) to match its risk.
- **3 quick wins:** remove the `beforeEach` import; switch the two tests' `'en'` select value to `'en-US'`; fix FEATURES_REQUIRED §7 and the undefined-`fname` MSG handler.
