## Summary
- **Counts:** Critical 0 · High 1 (carry-over) · Medium 3 (all NEW) · Low 7 (5 NEW, 2 carry-over).
- **Top 3 high-priority fixes:** (1) close the carry-over High — error code+params from `CoreApi` (incl. the two App.jsx German templates); (2) **free closed-window sessions** (`close_session` from the close guard) — the only runtime-degrading finding; (3) extract + test **`mergeableIds`** so the order fix can't regress.
- **Top 2 architectural improvements:** (1) an error-code contract between `CoreApi` and the SPA (UI owns wording); (2) an explicit symmetric session lifecycle in `CoreApi` (create/close release lock+path+variants+cache together) vs today's grow-only dicts.
- **3 quick wins:** add the 3 components to `i18n.test.js`; `aria-live` on error/notice + `aria-label` on icon buttons; gate the context-menu merge on selection-membership + `logger.exception` before the MotW dialog.
